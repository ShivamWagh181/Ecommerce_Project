Background of Owner & History

Mr. Vilas Umbarkar is owner of this company. He is from farmer family and his native place is Umbri Balapur, Tal -Sangamner. He was attracted towards this business in 1998 and started milk collection from other farmers and also with his own cow milk. He was supplying that collected milk to S.R Thorat and Sangamner Taluka Dudh Sangh at the beginning.

History - In July 2007 Mr. Umbarkar had purchased one small dairy unit at Nimgoan Jali which was previously not working. He has done some renovation work and get started all equipments. Initially he was collecting milk, chilling and then sale to other parties as bulk milk by tanker. In Jan 2008 he had done some investment for process milk plant and pouching equipments. He had started pouch milk sale in small volume and stopped bulk milk sale by increasing pouch milk sale. He was sailing pouch milk under brand Name ''Ranjan'' . He captured market in Maharashtra as well as in Gujarat. He had done this pouch milk business till 2021.

New Development - In 2015 he started thinking to start other milk product and ice cream. For one year he has done study of all milk products and ice cream which is available in market, equipments required for this, supplier of equipments, sizes of packaging, packing material suppliers etc. After sufficient study he has constructed building with good and hygienic design which is required for milk products. He purchased all equipments in the end of 2015. He has started Dahi(Curd), Lassi, Chhas (Butter Milk), Paneer, Shrikhand and ice cream in Feb 2016. All products under brand ''Ranjan'' was very good in quality and so this brand is accepted by consumers in market very quickly. Today in 2021 Ranjan is having good response from market. Their sales team is working in Marathwada, Vidarbha region of Maharashtra as well in Pune, Mumbai and some local market nearby Sangamner. These all product they are sailing in Surat, Ankaleshwar and Bharuch cities of Gujarat state. Today Jay Hanuman Milk and Milk Product Pvt Ltd handling average milk throughout year 25000 Lit per day. In which they are sailing 20000 Lit per day in pouches and remaining 5000 Lit per day they are using for all milk products. They have plan to grow in milk handling by 10-15 % every year.
